About Per Scholas
Unlocking Potential

At Per Scholas, we believe a thriving workforce starts with equitable access to education. Learn about Per Scholas and how we provide skills training and access to employer networks to individuals often excluded from tech careers. Per Scholas envisions a technology workforce as diverse as the customers it serves. Over 17,000 graduates have launched successful careers in tech to date through our no-cost technical training. mission is to advance economic equity through rigorous training for tech careers and to connect skilled talent to leading businesses.

Creating Opportunity for Careers in Tech
We work directly with employers, large and small, to design rigorous, boot camp-style courses with an 85% graduation rate. Our tuition-free training combines business professional skills development with hands-on technical instruction for high demand jobs.
